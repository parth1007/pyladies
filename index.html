Who are we?

PyLadies IIIT Allahabad, is a social group for female Python coders and developers.
We are affiliated with the global PyLadies mentorship group.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, workshops, events and social gatherings.

Events

We organise meetups which are followed by a short talk and workshop formula.
Our Meetup group provides with all the necessary details for the upcoming events.

Prerequisite?

No matter whether you are a Python novice or an expert or anywhere in between, we encourage everyone to join our next meetup.
